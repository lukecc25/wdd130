Personal Safety List:

1. Please Bring Water
2. Wear sunscrean/protection from sun
3. Bring a change of clothes
4. Eat before coming as there will be no food on the river
5. Don't come alone, make sure you have a companion that can keep an eye on you
6. Find a life vest that fits
7. Find a helmet that fits

Raft Safety List:

1. Keep hand on the T-shaped end of the oar at all times
2. Don't jump out of the raft unless permitted by guide.
3. If you fall out of the raft and are seperated from raft then backstroke towards the shore.
4. If you're stuck in the rapids with an obstacle coming your way then try to get on top of it. DO NOT GO UNDER IT!!!
5. Keep feet planted and secured in the edges of the raft. This will give you more stability when in the rapids.

Please Don't:

1. Push people out of the raft
2. Feed the Sasquatch
3. Relax while everyone is rowing
4. Hit others with your oar
